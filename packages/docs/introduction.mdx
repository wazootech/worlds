---

Worlds is an open-source context engine designed to manage, query, update, and
reason over interoperable knowledge bases at the edge.

At its core, Worlds is an indexed knowledge graph. It provides agents with a
deterministic context graph that offers complete explainability and source
provenance, ensuring continually improving output quality and traceable
reasoning.
