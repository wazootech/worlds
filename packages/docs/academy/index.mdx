Welcome to the **Worlds Academy**.

The industry is currently obsessed with increasing model size, but Worlds
focuses on increasing the intelligence of the **environment** those models
inhabit. This curriculum is designed to take you from a curious developer to an
architect of stateful, self-improving AI ecosystems.

## The curriculum

The Academy is organized into modular chapters, each focusing on a fundamental
pillar of the Worlds infrastructure.

## How to learn

Each lesson in this academy is designed to be standalone but follows a logical
progression. We recommend following the chapters in order if you are new to RDF
and neuro-symbolic AI.
