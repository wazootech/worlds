---
title: "Code of Conduct"
---

Worlds is committed to fostering a welcoming, professional, and inclusive
community. Everyone must adhere to these standards.

## Community standards

- **Be respectful**: Treat others with dignity and kindness.
- **Be professional**: Focus on technical excellence and collaborative
  problem-solving.
- **Be inclusive**: Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels.
- **Be direct**: Provide clear, constructive feedback without editorializing.

Harassment, discrimination, or any form of exclusionary behavior is not
tolerated. If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, report it to the
maintainers immediately.

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right and responsibility to remove or edit content that
does not align with this Code of Conduct, and to suspend or ban members for
serious violations.
